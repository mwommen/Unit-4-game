UFC 239 Guessing Game

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four fighters below. By clicking on a fighter you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your score is above the random number.
  4. The value of each fighter is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game resets, the values of each fighter will also change.
